Welcome to Connivance: an interactive mystery

Something strange is happening in the small town of Connivance, Missouri. After the mysterious slaughter of prized cattle, unidentified footprints nearby, and a strange figure lurking in the woods the Sheriff seems clueless. With the townspeople living in fear and your newspaper editor breathing down your neck to snag the next big headline, do you have what it takes to uncover the mystery?

This experience is an exploration of truth, ideation, curiosity, and empathy in a world where the facts are not always what they seem.

Rose Bender

When Rose was 14, she discovered the "View Source" function in her browser, designed her first LiveJournal layout*, and never looked back.

With more than 10 years of professional experience, she is passionate about helping non-profits, artists, and entrepreneurs build community through effective storytelling and meaningful digital experiences.

She is hands-on and enjoys learning by doing. As a result, she's done a bit of everything–from magazine production, to writing copy, to filming video, to developing integrated strategy–the list goes on. Her expertise lies in content production, website management, email marketing, and holistic digital problem solving.

She earned her BS in Business Administration from Drexel University, with a focus on marketing and a minor in communications. in 2019 she received her MS in Integrated Digital Media from NYU's Tandon School of Engineering.


Outside of work, Rose is a cyclist, traveler, reader, camper, concert-goer, and planner of get togethers.
